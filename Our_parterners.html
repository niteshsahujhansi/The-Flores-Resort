Bouchard Finlayson

Our sister vineyard lies in the lovely Hemel-en-Aarde Valley (Heaven and Earth), part of the Walker Bay wine region near Hermanus, South Africa’s whale watching capital. We are proud to serve Bouchard Finlayson, acclaimed worldwide for producing the best Pinot Noir in South Africa, as well as exceptional Chardonnays and Sauvignon Blancs.

Champagne Lanson

As one of the oldest holders of the Royal Warrant, Lanson has been producing some of the world’s finest champagnes since 1760 and the craft has been passed down from generation to generation. We are proud to serve Lanson Champagne in our restaurant and bar.

BELU Water

Providing The Milestone with sustainable glass-bottled mineral water as well as filtered water, BELU supports us in our bid to eliminate single-use plastic bottles. BELU is one of our proud partners and gives 100% of their profits to the charity WaterAid to help transform lives worldwide with clean water.

Leitha CleanCoat™

In partnership with Leitha CleanCoat™, we use this effective and sustainable self-disinfection solution at The Milestone, further improving the wellbeing of our staff and guests.

Pascal Lavorel

Pascal Lavorel and his team are a dedicated group of florists and gardeners. They design exciting floral displays, exquisite classical bouquets and arrangements which take centre stage in our hotel.

Maiden Voyage

Maiden Voyage is dedicated to the needs of female travellers by recommending female friendly hotels and services; connecting women who are travelling; and providing useful travel and safety information. We are proud to meet their hotel selection criteria.

The RPO

In partnership with the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra, we are delighted to offer our guests the opportunity to enjoy a live orchestral performance in the privacy of your suite.

H Forman & Son

The Forman family has been curing and smoking fish in the East End of London since 1905 and is the last of the original London salmon smokeries. We are proud to serve this delicious smoked salmon in our restaurant.

PMD Tea

PMD Tea is a family owned business, running for over 75 years and through three generations. At the centre of all PMD Tea is the time-honoured tradition of skilfully hand picking two leaves and a bud, producing a signature flavour that can be enjoyed with our delicious Afternoon Teas.
