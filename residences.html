A private home in the city

Combine the value, space and authenticity of renting a private home with the service, facilities and security of a five-star hotel. Our six luxury two or three-bedroom residences are perfect for extended stays or when travelling with family and friends, comfortably accommodating up to 5 - 6 guests.

Located opposite Kensington Palace, each residence has direct access through the hotel or may be accessed via your own private entrance. Enjoy 24-hour room service, twice-daily housekeeping service, full concierge service, complimentary use of Health Club, grocery shopping service and use of our chauffeur.

Our Residences are designed for a maximum stay of three months. Should you wish to stay longer, we can look to roll on your agreement whilst you are here at the hotel. Please feel free to discuss any special requirements with our Reservations team.

Welcome hamper

When you arrive, our carefully curated complimentary hamper of treats will be waiting in your Residence, including biscuits, macarons, mixed nuts and chocolates, all created by our Executive Chef Daniel Putz and his team. The hamper will also showcase Cheddar Gorge cheese straws, English Breakfast Tea from our long-standing partner PMD Teas and a bottle of “Hannibal” red wine from our sister wine estate Bouchard Finlayson, presented with a handwritten scroll from our Butler.

Your kitchen will also be stocked with red and green apples, orange and apple juice, whole and semi-skimmed milk, a selection of cereals, unlimited PMD Tea and illy coffee, a sliced sourdough bloomer, butter, bottled water, homemade preserves and Mrs T’s famous Cape seed loaf, with our compliments.
