The Victoria & Albert

Located on the second floor, the elegant Victoria & Albert residence conveys a calm and peaceful ambience, while the traditional design mirrors the rich history of the building with luxury fabrics, antique furniture and spectacular artwork. It showcases an open plan kitchen and spacious entertaining area, as well as a lovely outlook onto the street. It is an ideal family option with a sofa bed plus two thoughtfully designed bedrooms with king-size beds.

Residence size: 82.8m2

Capacity: Two adults and up to four children or four adults and up to two small children
